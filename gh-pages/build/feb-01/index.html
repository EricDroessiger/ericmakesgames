    About Me

    Hello, I'm Eric Drößiger.
    My Goal as a Game Designer is to create games that bring out the player's intrinsic playfulness and give them a sense of adventure.

    Since 2016 I'm studying International Game Architecture and Design (IGAD) at Breda University of Applied Sciences in the Netherlands (formerly NHTV Breda).

    In the first two years of my study, I started as a general Designer, expanding my skills in various Game Design and Production aspects. Now in my third year, I'm specializing in Tech Design.

    Before my study, I got the opportunity to work a year as a supporting team lead at ka:media interactive in Düsseldorf. This helped me gain valuable experience in a leadership position which came in helpful during University projects.

    One of my big hobbies is learning Japanese. The culture and language fascinated me since I started learning it 5 years ago. Besides this, I love playing Multiplayer games, due to my satisfaction when overcoming a challenge or building something together with my friends.
