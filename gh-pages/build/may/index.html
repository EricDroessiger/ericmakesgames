    About Me

    Hello, I'm Eric Drößiger.
    I love playing and making games.

    What I love most about creating games is bringing out intrinsic playfulness and creating a sense of adventure that makes people smile. Something that I got to love as well is working together with the open and diverse people in the game industry.

    Since 2016 I'm studying International Game Architecture and Design (IGAD) at Breda University of Applied Sciences in the Netherlands (formerly NHTV Breda). Now in my third Univerity year, I'm working on an unannounced year-long Nintendo Switch project in a team of 22 people.

    I'm currently specializing as a Tech Designer. I enjoy and feel comfortable at prototyping and implementing game systems and mechanics. Problem-solving can be hard but it is a thrill to create something working. I'm also enthusiastic about other areas of game development like Level Design, UI/UX Design, Game Feel and System Design.

    Before my study, I got the opportunity to work a year as a team lead at ka:media interactive in Düsseldorf. This helped me gain valuable experience in a leadership position but most importantly improved my skills for giving applicable feedback. This experience came in helpful during University projects.

    I love playing games, especially Multiplayer games due to the satisfaction when overcoming a challenge together with my friends. One of my big hobbies besides gaming is learning Japanese.
